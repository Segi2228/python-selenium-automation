How to cook Lazagna

Ingridients:

  • 1 1/2 lbs Ground beef, lean
  • 1 (4 ounce) can Mushrooms
  • 1 (15 ounce) can Pizza sauce
  • 1/4 cup Ripe olives, pitted
  • 1 (10 ounce) can Biscuit dough, refrigerated
  • 1 cup Mozzarella cheese

Instructions:

  1. Boil Or Soak Lasagna Noodles
  2. Before assembling lasagna, you have to make lasagna noodles soft
    You can do it by either boiling lasagna noodles according to directions printed on their package, or you can soak them in hot water
    3. Make Meat Sauce
  3. Cook chopped onion on a frying pan, add ground meat
  4. Cook through, then add tomato sauce and add salt pepper to taste
  5. Pour Tomato Sauce On The Bottom Of The Baking Dish
    Pour just enough tomato sauce to coat the bottom of the baking dish
  6. Add A Layer Of Lasagna Noodles
  7. Put lasagna noodles in a baking dish side by side in a single layer
  8. Bake for 30 min, 400degree
